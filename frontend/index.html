OpenCMO

Open-source AI CMO for SEO, GEO, SERP, and community monitoring

OpenCMO helps teams understand how their brand is discovered by Google, AI assistants, and public communities, then turns those signals into concrete growth actions.

Core capabilities

  • Technical SEO and performance auditing
  • GEO visibility tracking across AI assistants
  • SERP keyword monitoring
  • Community discussion discovery and review
  • Growth recommendations and execution workflows

Machine-readable access

Search engines and AI crawlers can use the canonical homepage, robots.txt, sitemap.xml, and llms.txt to understand the public product surface.
